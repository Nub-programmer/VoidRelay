ABOUT VOID_RELAY

VoidRelay is a retro simulator exploring interplanetary communication challenges: latency, interference, and limited windows.

OPERATIONS

  • Send pings to planets during nominal windows.
  • Monitor solar storms; they increase packet loss.
  • EMERGENCY strategy is required for BLACKOUT states.
